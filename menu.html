Frequently Asked Questions

What are your opening hours? +

We're open Monday to Thursday from 11am to 10pm, Friday and Saturday from 11am to 11pm, and Sunday from 12pm to 9pm. We're here to serve you delicious burgers throughout the week!

Do you offer vegetarian or vegan options? +

Yes! We offer a delicious Veggie Supreme burger made with a house-made plant-based patty. We can also customize most burgers to be vegetarian-friendly. Please let our staff know about any dietary requirements.

Can I customize my burger? +

Absolutely! We encourage you to customize your burger to your liking. Add extra toppings, swap sauces, or remove ingredients - just let us know your preferences when ordering.

Do you offer delivery? +

Yes! We partner with major delivery platforms including GrabFood, Deliveroo, and Foodpanda. You can also order directly through our website for pickup or delivery within a 5km radius.

Are your burgers halal-certified? +

We are currently working towards halal certification. All our beef and chicken are sourced from halal-certified suppliers, but we have not yet received our official certification. Please check with us for the latest updates.

Do you cater for events and parties? +

Yes! We offer catering services for events, parties, and corporate functions. Contact us at catering@burgs.sg or call us to discuss your catering needs and we'll create a custom menu for your event.
