Our Story

Born from a passion for perfecting the craft of burger-making, Burgs started as a dream in a small kitchen and has grown into Singapore's most beloved burger destination. We believe that a great burger is more than just food—it's an experience that brings people together, creates memories, and satisfies cravings like nothing else can.

Every burger we create is a testament to our commitment to quality, flavor, and innovation. From our handcrafted beef patties to our homemade buns, signature sauces, and carefully selected toppings, we pour our heart into every single element. We source only the finest ingredients, work with trusted suppliers, and never compromise on taste or freshness.

Our journey has been fueled by the incredible support of our community—the burger lovers who line up at our door, share their experiences, and keep coming back for more. We're not just serving burgers; we're building a movement of flavor enthusiasts who believe that food should be bold, exciting, and unforgettable. Welcome to the Burgs family.

Our Journey

2019
The Beginning
Burgs was founded with a simple mission: to create the perfect burger. Starting from a humble home kitchen, we experimented with recipes, perfected our techniques, and dreamed big.
2020
First Pop-Up
We launched our first pop-up stall, serving burgers at local markets and events. The response was overwhelming—people loved our bold flavors and quality ingredients.
2021
Permanent Location
We opened our first brick-and-mortar restaurant, bringing our signature burgers to hungry fans every single day. Lines formed, reviews poured in, and we knew we were onto something special.
2023
Award Recognition
Burgs was recognized as one of Singapore's top burger joints, winning multiple awards for our innovative menu and exceptional quality. Our community grew stronger than ever.
2024
Expansion & Innovation
We expanded our menu with new creations like the Smokin Burg and Double Trouble, introduced catering services, and continued pushing the boundaries of what a burger can be.
2025
The Future
With new locations in the works and exciting menu innovations on the horizon, we're just getting started. The best burgers are yet to come.
